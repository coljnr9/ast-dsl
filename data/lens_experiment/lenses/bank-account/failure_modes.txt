### 2. Irreversible States (Permanent Transitions)
*   **Contract Formation:** Once the institution accepts the application, the terms and conditions form a binding contract.
*   **Property Transfer:** Upon deposit, the actual cash "ceases to be the property of the depositor" and becomes the property of the bank. This is a permanent legal transition of ownership in exchange for a claim.
*   **Account Number Assignment:** Once an ID is assigned and transactions are recorded, it cannot be reused for a different entity/history (implied by the audit nature of bank statements).

### 3. State-Locked Operations (Impossible Actions)
*   **Lending from EMIs:** Electronic Money Institutions are "not permitted to use client funds for lending." This operation is physically/legally impossible within that specific system state.
*   **Withdrawal from Time Deposits:** Funds in a "Time Deposit (Bond)" or "Fixed Deposit" are locked for a duration; early withdrawal is usually impossible or violates terms.
*   **Standard Deposit Insurance on EMIs:** EMI accounts cannot trigger deposit insurance schemes as they are not "deposit-taking institutions."

### 4. Edge Cases (Condition Combinations)
*   **Balance Polarity Switch:** Accounts that can "switch between credit and debit balances." A failure occurs if the system logic for interest calculation does not flip instantly (charging interest vs. paying interest).
*   **Cross-Border Multi-Currency:** When "multi-currency" and "international transactions" occur, a failure in exchange rate synchronization can create an overdraft in one currency while the total balance is positive.
*   **Safeguarding Failure:** EMIs must safeguard funds with "third-party credit institutions." If the third party fails, the EMI account holder faces a "High-Risk" loss scenario not present in traditional banks.

### 5. Out of Scope (Unsupported Operations)
*   **Direct Bailment:** The system does *not* support keeping the original physical currency for the customer (it is not a safe-deposit box).
*   **Anonymous Accounts:** While "Numbered bank accounts" exist, "false names" or anonymous operation is generally listed as unlawful/unsupported.
*   **EMI Interest Generation:** EMIs generally do not pay interest on "idle balances" as they are prohibited from lending those funds.
