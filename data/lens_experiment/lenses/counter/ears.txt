2. **While** RESET is TRUE, the **CTU** shall set Q to FALSE.

4. **When** RESET changes from FALSE to TRUE, the **CTU** shall set CV to 0.
5. **Whenever** CV is greater than or equal to PV, the **CTU** shall set Q to TRUE.
6. **Whenever** CV is less than PV, the **CTU** shall set Q to FALSE.

8. **Complex:** While RESET is TRUE, when CU has a rising edge, the **CTU** shall maintain CV at 0.
