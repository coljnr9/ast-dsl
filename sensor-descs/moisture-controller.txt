Write a domain model of a soil moisture monitoring and irrigation controller. The system reads a moisture level from a sensor (an integer percentage 0-100). A target moisture threshold is configurable — when the moisture reading falls below the threshold, watering should activate. The system operates in two modes: automatic (watering activates and deactivates based on the threshold comparison) and manual (the operator directly controls watering on/off, ignoring the threshold). Switching from manual to auto mode causes the system to immediately evaluate the threshold condition. The system tracks whether it is currently watering, how many watering cycles have completed, and the cumulative watering duration. A watering cycle begins when watering activates and ends when it deactivates. Reading moisture while no sensor is connected is undefined. The controller can be reset to its initial state: auto mode, threshold at 50%, no active watering, zero cycle count.
